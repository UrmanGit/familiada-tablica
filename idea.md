# Familiada board:

---

## Normal mode

In normal mode, the operator has the ability to switch between current teams.
While a team is selected, the operator has to show answers provided by the team, and if their answer is not there, he should give them the "X".
The answers are numbered `1–6`, and the "X" is given by the `x` key.

## Finale mode

In the finale, there are 6 questions that the team leader has to answer.
If his answer is in the answers, the operator presses a key and the board switches to another question.
After all questions are answered, the operator — by pressing `enter`, shows the answer and the points it has assigned.
